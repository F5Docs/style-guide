# Language and spelling

## Guidance

Use American English spelling throughout. When a word isn't covered here or in [word-list](./word-list.md), use the [American Heritage Dictionary](https://ahdictionary.com/) as the spelling reference.

## Examples

**Do:**
> organize

> color

> license (noun and verb)

> canceled

**Don't:**
> organise

> colour

> licence

> cancelled
