# Dates and times

## Guidance

Use month/day/year format for dates. Use lowercase a.m./p.m. with a
space before them for times. Always specify the time zone for events.

## Examples

**Do:**
> June 17, 2026

> 10 a.m. PT

> The event runs from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. ET.

**Don't:**
> 17 June 2026

> 10AM

> 10:00 AM PT

> The event runs from 9-5.

## Notes

Date format:
- Always use month name, not numerals: June 17, 2026 not 6/17/2026
- Don't use ordinals: June 17, not June 17th

Time format:
- Use lowercase with periods: a.m. and p.m.
- Include a space before a.m./p.m.: 10 a.m. not 10am
- Always specify the time zone for scheduled events

Recurring availability:
- Use 24x7, not 24/7 (F5 standard)

Version ranges:
- Use "earlier" and "later" for version comparisons, not
  "before/after," "above/below," "greater/lower"
- Use "through" for inclusive ranges in prose: BIG-IP 12.1 through 12.4
- Use a hyphen for ranges in tables and titles: 12.1-12.4
